Ninjago Seasons Summary

Season 1: Rise of the Snakes

The ninja face their greatest threat yet with the return of Lord Garmadon and the rise of the Hypnobrai and other Serpentine tribes.

Season 2: Legacy of the Green Ninja

Lloyd Garmadon discovers his destiny as the Green Ninja while the ninja battle the powerful Overlord and the Nindroids.

Season 3: Rebooted

The ninja enter a digital realm to stop the Overlord's plans for a technological takeover, facing new enemies and challenges.

Season 4: Tournament of Elements

The ninja compete in the Tournament of Elements to rescue their friends and stop Master Chen's plans with the elemental fighters.

Season 5: Possession

Cole becomes possessed by the ghost of Yang, leading the ninja to confront ghosts and spirits from their past in a battle to save Ninjago.

Season 6: Skybound

Jay makes a deal with the djinn Nadakhan, leading to unintended consequences as he uses his wishes to reshape Ninjago.

Season 7: Hands of Time

The ninja face the Time Twins, Krux and Acronix, who seek to rewrite history and take control of Ninjago using their mastery of time.

Season 8: Sons of Garmadon

The ninja confront the dangerous Sons of Garmadon cult, who seek to resurrect Lord Garmadon and plunge Ninjago into darkness.

Season 9: Hunted

The ninja are hunted by Emperor Garmadon and the Sons of Garmadon, leading to a showdown in the dangerous realm of Oni and Dragons.

Season 10: March of the Oni

The ninja face the Oni invasion, battling to protect Ninjago from the dark forces threatening to destroy their world.

Season 11: Secrets of the Forbidden Spinjitzu

The ninja uncover ancient secrets and powerful new forms of Spinjitzu as they protect Ninjago from new and old threats alike.
